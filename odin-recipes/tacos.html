Tacos

Time : 45 mins

This recipe will teach you how to make Chicken Tacos, it will take about 45 minutes to make!

Tacos are great, everyone loves them! They are a quick and easy dish to make that even the beginner chef should be able to rustle up

In the past, I have also replaced the chicken with a meat free alternative. If you decide to do this, please use the cooking advice that is suggested on the packaging. Just don't forget the seasoning!

You will need the following:

  • 300g chicken breast fillets
  • Grated Cheddar
  • Taco seasoning
  • lettuce
  • Taco seasoning
  • Small wraps
  • sour cream

Steps:

  1. Place chicken on a baking tray and cover with taco seasoning
  2. Cook chicken for 35 minutes at 200C
  3. Once cooked, cut chicken into smaller pieces
  4. Layer sauce onto empty wrap, add chicken, lettuce and cheese
  5. Enjoy!!!

I hope you enjoyed my taco recipe, I use it all the time!
