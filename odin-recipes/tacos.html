Tacos/h1>

Time : 45 mins

This recipe will teach you how to make Chicken Tacos, it will take about 45 minutes to make!

You will need the following:

  • 300g chicken breast fillets
  • Grated Cheddar
  • Taco seasoning
  • lettuce
  • Taco seasoning
  • Small wraps
  • sour cream

Steps:

  1. Place chicken on a baking tray and cover with taco seasoning
  2. Cook chicken for 35 minutes at 200C
  3. Once cooked, cut chicken into smaller pieces
  4. Layer sauce onto empty wrap, add chicken, lettuce and cheese
  5. Enjoy!!!
