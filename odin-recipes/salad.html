Salad

Time : 10 mins

This recipe will teach you how to make Salad, it will take about 10 minutes to make!

Salad is a quick and easy healthy dish and the best part is, you can add whatever ingredients you like!

I really enjoy making salad as I can make it super quick and add in all the ingredients I want. For this recipe, I will be adding ham, cheese, radishes, peppers and more! Don't like any of the ingredients I'm using? swap them out for something else!

You will need the following:

  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar
  • Peppers
  • Sliced ham
  • Radishes
  • Caesar dressing

Steps:

  1. Cut up cheddar into small bite size cubes
  2. Slice peppers into small squares
  3. Cut radishes in halves
  4. Add all ingredients into container and pour dressing on top then mix
  5. Enjoy!!!

I hope you enjoyed my salad recipe, I use it all the time!
